The author, Pierre Dujols, reinterprets Chivalry not as a mere military code, but as a sophisticated esoteric school preserving ancient pagan wisdom.

Dujols traces the etymology to the Egyptian word *Gradal*, a vessel used to hold sacred fire. The Quest for the Grail is thus reinterpreted as an alchemical or spiritual quest for the inner divine spark, or "fire," a concept central to the project's exploration of subtle energy.
