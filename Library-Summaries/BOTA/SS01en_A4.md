**`Library/BOTA/SS01en_A4.pdf` (`Seven Steps in Practical Occultism - Lesson 1: Builders of the Adytum`)**:
    *   **Relevance:** This lesson provides the absolute first principle for the practical operator of any "consciousness technology": the formulation of a precise, unwavering intention. It moves directly from theory into a required practical exercise, establishing the disciplined methodology that underpins the entire Unified Hermetic Framework.
    *   **Key Contributions:**
        *   **The Technology of Intention:** The lesson's primary focus is on the "magic of a real want." It requires the student to distill their desire into a single, written sentence. This act of clarification is presented not as a mere psychological trick, but as a foundational act of "setting in order" or "regulating" one's own powers to harmonize with the universal order. This directly corresponds to the creation of a "rate" in Radionics, which is a symbolic representation of a desired outcome.
        *   **The Universal Agent ("Spiritus Mundi"):** The lesson introduces Eliphas Levi's "Great Magical Agent," describing it as a "fluid and a perpetual vibration" that is the "substance of heaven and earth." This is a clear description of the "Spiritus Mundi." The text explicitly states that this force is directed by "the will of intelligent beings" and can be used to "heal or hurt at a distance," providing a direct theoretical basis for the action of Psionics and Radionics.
        *   **Power is Inherent, Not Acquired:** A core principle is established: the student does not need to *get* power, but must learn to *use* the immense power they already possess. The work is about directing the "hidden force" that "always responds to your mental direction." This empowers the operator, framing them not as a supplicant, but as the master of their own inner world and, by extension, their environment.
        *   **The "Adytum" as the Perfected Human:** The goal is framed as "building the Adytum" (the inner shrine), which means transforming the human organism—body, emotions, and mind—into a perfected instrument capable of consciously directing the universal power. This aligns with the Framework's concept of the biophysics of the human energy field and its role in consciousness technology.
